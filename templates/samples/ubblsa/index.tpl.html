BLSA Executive Board Vacancies

The Executive Board is happy to announce that we have filled the vacancy of Historian with Tashani Dickson. We are happy to have Tashani join our Board for the academic year!

There are a few BLSA Executive Board positions that were not filled in this year's election. They include the position of Secretary, and Parliamentarian.

If you are interested in serving in one of these positions and joining our BLSA Executive Board for next year, please email to President, Brandon Cahee a statement of interest of (no more than one page) why you are interested in serving in such capacity as soon as possible. *Please make sure to include some innovative ideas you may want to bring to the Board if selected for the particular position that aligns to the position that you are applying for.*

The BLSA Board will review all statements of interests received and our President, Brandon Cahee will recommend based on the statements of interest a selection for approval by 2/3 vote of the Executive Board. Upon confirmation of vote, the BLSA Executive Board will alert by email all positions that have been filled.

We hope that you will consider applying as a candidate for any of the open vacancies that we have!
